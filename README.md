# 🚗 Car Sales Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Car Sales Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze car sales performance and provide valuable business insights. The dashboard helps users monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify sales trends, compare regional performance, and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective

- Analyze overall car sales performance.
- Track monthly and yearly sales trends.
- Monitor total revenue and total cars sold.
- Compare sales across different regions and dealers.
- Provide interactive business insights using Power BI.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- Power BI
- Microsoft Excel
- Power Query
- DAX

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes the following information:

- Car Model
- Brand
- Dealer Name
- Region
- Sales Date
- Selling Price
- Quantity Sold
- Customer Details
- Payment Method

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features

- 📌 Total Sales KPI
- 💰 Total Revenue
- 🚗 Total Cars Sold
- 📈 Monthly Sales Trend
- 🏷️ Brand-wise Sales Analysis
- 🌍 Region-wise Sales Analysis
- 🏢 Dealer-wise Performance
- 🎛️ Interactive Slicers & Filters

---

## 📈 Key Insights

- Identified top-selling car brands.
- Compared sales performance across regions.
- Analyzed monthly sales trends.
- Tracked dealer performance.
- Improved business decision-making using interactive dashboards.
